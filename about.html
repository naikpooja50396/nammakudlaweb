About Namma Kudla

Your trusted companion for exploring Mangalore

Our Story

Namma Kudla was born from a passion for Mangalore and a desire to share its hidden treasures with the world. We are a community of travelers, locals, and enthusiasts who believe that Mangalore has so much more to offer than what meets the eye.

Our Mission

We aim to be the go-to platform for anyone looking to explore Mangalore. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a lifelong resident, our blog provides authentic experiences, insider tips, and comprehensive guides to help you discover the best of what Kudla has to offer.
