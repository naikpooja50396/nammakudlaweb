About Namma Kudla

The Heart of Coastal Karnataka

Kudla, affectionately known as Mangalore, is a vibrant coastal city that beautifully blends tradition with modernity. Nestled between the Western Ghats and the Arabian Sea, this port city is renowned for its stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, and mouthwatering cuisine.

From the historic temples and churches that dot the landscape to the bustling fish markets and serene backwaters, Kudla offers a unique experience for every traveler. The warm hospitality of the locals, known as Kudlites, makes every visitor feel at home.
